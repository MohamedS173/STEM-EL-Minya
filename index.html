Welcome in STEM Elmenya

Overview

An excellent education is offered at STEM Elmenya, a public high school in Egypt, to pupils who are driven by academics.

STEM schools cater to high-potential kids who have excelled in the classroom and offer a distinctive system that concentrates on the scientific, mathematical, and technological sectors.

ِAbout us.

mission

1. Provide students with a safe healthy learning and living environment

2. Help students develop their scientific research, academic and social skills

3. Encourage all stakeholders’ active and productive participation and collaboration

4. Develop staff and student’s commitment to moral rules, social values and national standards

5. Keep up with the latest trends in learning and teaching STEM areas worldwide

vision

We at STEM Elmenya High School think the institution was intended to produce "centres of excellence" that represent cross-disciplinary cooperation, practical application, critical thinking, and a spirit of innovation. In the end, STEM institutions foster a new perception of Egypt as a nation having the institutional and human resources necessary to compete and succeed in the global economy. In order to educate high-achieving students from across Egypt, regardless of socioeconomic status, the schools' main objective is to establish a network of self-sufficient schools with newly established curricula, qualified teachers, industry partnerships, and an operational system under the MOE's public schooling system.

Admission.

With approximately 150 students in each grade, admitted applicants are selected through a competitive admission process with an admission criterion that selects students based upon their grade 9 scores and a special admission test and recommendations from prep schools. Moreover, admission criteria evaluate and consider prospective students’ demonstrated aptitude and passion for studies in the scientific, mathematical, and technological fields. With application requirements including A+ as a total grade in his middle school examination, A* on two only of math, science and English tests. The applicants are further assessed on a national test day using IQ tests and both math and science tests. The admissions pool is highly selective as the school receives more than 10000 applications annually. Each year, approximately 150 students are offered admission to the incoming 10th grade class. The enrollment for the 2020 - 2021- school year is 75. No tuition or residence cost is required for students; The Ministry supports the school financially.
